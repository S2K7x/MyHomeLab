## Hardware Configuration: Beelink Mini S12

I am using a **Beelink Mini S12**, a mini PC featuring:

* **Processor:** Intel Twin Lake-N100 (up to 3.4 GHz)
* **Memory:** 16 GB RAM
* **Storage:** 500 GB SSD
* **Connectivity:** 4K Dual Display (HDMI), Wi-Fi 6, Bluetooth 5.2, and Dual 2.5G LAN

### Software & Installation

* **Operating System:** It is running **Proxmox VE**.
* [Proxmox ISO Download](https://www.proxmox.com/en/downloads/proxmox-virtual-environment/iso)


* **Installation Method:** I flashed the USB drive using **BalenaEtcher**.
* [BalenaEtcher Website](https://etcher.balena.io/)
